Tour Locations & Stories

  • St Andrew’s Church: Victorian Gothic, haunted organ notes, chill in the nave.
  • Temple Street Night Market: Neon-lit, fortune tellers, shadowless clients.
  • Tin Hau Temple (Yau Ma Tei): Goddess of the Sea, spirits of drowned sailors.
  • Yau Ma Tei Theatre: Phantom applause, flickering lights.
  • Shanghai Street (Tong Lau Row): Pre-war shophouses, wartime echoes, lullabies.
  • Mei Ho House: Resettlement estate, roaming spirits.
  • Man Fat Seafood Restaurant: Dai pai dong, Hungry Ghost Festival feasts.
